Gluten-free pancakes

Ideal as a sports breakfast or as a pre and post workout snack, any other flavorings of your choice added will make it taste just better.

High-protein gluten-free and lactose-free bodybuilding pancakes recipe

For a good full bodybuilding breakfast.

Preparation time: 4 mins

Cooking time: 5 mins

American cuisine

For 1 person

Ingredients:

  • 50g Buckwheat powder or gluten-free oats or sweet potato
  • 20g protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon gluten-free yeast (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon almond butter cinnamon or sucralose or stevia (optional)
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon coconut fat

Preparation of the recipe:

  1. Mix all the ingredients in the blender.
  2. Heat a skillet over high heat for 2 minutes.
  3. Lower the heat to 1/4 power and add a teaspoon of coconut fat.
  4. Put half a ladle of pancake batter.
  5. When small bubbles are present, turn the pancake.
  6. Taste.

Nutritional values:

Calories 473kcal
Pootein 39g
carbohydrates 40g
Lipids 3g
