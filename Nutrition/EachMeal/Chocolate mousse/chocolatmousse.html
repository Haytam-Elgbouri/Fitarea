Chocolate Mousse

Chocolate mousse just might be the ultimate chocolate fix! Rich and creamy, yet light and fluffy, one pot is satisfying but always leaves you wanting more.This is a classic American made chocolate mousse, as served in fine dining restaurants.Less cream, more chocolate, more intense chocolate flavor and a nice creamy mouthfeel.

Diet chocolate mousse recipe

A recipe for chocolate mousse with no added sugars or fats and delicious.

Preparation time: 15 mins

Total time: 6 hours

French diet cuisine

For 6 people

Ingredients:

  • 5 egg whites
  • 180 g of dark chocolate between 70 and 90% cocoa
  • A pinch of salt

Preparation of the recipe:

  1. Melt the chocolate in a saucepan with a little water for a smooth and creamy texture.
  2. Beat the egg whites until stiff with a mixer for a solid texture.
  3. Add the pinch of salt to the chocolate.
  4. Gently fold the chocolate into the egg whites little by little (the wooden spoon is ideal to avoid breaking the whites).
  5. Put the preparation in 6 mini-ramekins.
  6. Reserve in the fridge for at least 6 hours.
  7. Enjoy as is or with a little grated coconut on top.

Nutritional values:

Calories 192kcal
Protein 5g
carbohydrates 14g
Lipids 7g
