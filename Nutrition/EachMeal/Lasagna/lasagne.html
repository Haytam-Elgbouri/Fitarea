Lasagna

Craving some lasagna, but don't want to make a whole platter of it? Try lasagna in a loaf tin! You'll get 4 easy servings.

Protein lasagna

This loaf tin lasagne makes the ultimate high-protein meal prep for when you’re craving your favourite comfort food.

Preparation time: 5 mins

Total time: 40min

Italian cuisine

For 4 people

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp. Coconut Oil
  • 1 white onion, coarsely chopped (or 1 tbsp. onion powder)
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped (or 1 tsp. garlic powder)
  • 1 tbsp. dried oregano
  • 350g turkey mince
  • 600g chopped tomatoes or tomato passata
  • 300g lasagne sheets
  • 1 courgette
  • 1 tsp. sea salt and black pepper
  • 400g cottage cheese
  • 3 egg whites
  • 100g low-fat cheese (grated)

Preparation of the recipe:

  1. Make your turkey ragu
  2. Add the coconut oil to a pan on a medium to high heat.
  3. Add the onion and sauté for 3-4 minutes, then add the garlic and sauté for a further 2 minutes (if you’re using the powdered versions, add them after the next step).
  4. Add the turkey mince and break it up a little using a spatula, then allow it to brown for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Stir in the oregano, ½ tsp
  6. Salt and pepper and the tomatoes and simmer on a low heat for 10 minutes.
  7. While you’re waiting, whisk cottage cheese and egg whites together in a bowl using a fork with the remaining salt and pepper. Set aside,and preheat the oven at 200°C or 180°C for fan-assisted.
  8. Prepare your courgette and lasagne sheets. Use a vegetable peeler to slice the courgette lengthways to get long slices.
  9. Wash the lasagne sheets under cold water in a colander.
  10. Once the turkey ragu is ready, it’s time to make up the lasagne. Start with a layer of courgette sheets for easy removal once cooked. Then alternate between the ragu, cheese sauce, lasagne sheets, and courgette. Finish with a layer of lasagne, then cheese sauce, then sprinkle with low-fat cheese.
  11. Bake for 15 minutes with foil on, then remove the foil, turn the heat up by 20°C and bake for a further 20 minutes. Once cooked, divide into four meal prep containers, serve with your favourite salad or vegetables and store in the fridge for up to three days.

Nutritional values:

Calories 555kcal
Protein 46g
carbohydrates 67g
Lipids 13g
