Salmon salad recipe

An easy-to-carry recipe in a Tupperware or other plastic box. Packed with carbohydrates, this recipe is to be consumed after bodybuilding training, this will allow you to quickly replenish glycogen reserves.

Salmon pasta salad

This dish is loaded with carbohydrates, and if eaten after strength training, it will quickly replenish glycogen stores.

Preparation time: 5 mins

Total time: 40min

Scandinavian cuisine

For 1 person

Ingredients:

  • 50g of salmon
  • 250g pasta
  • 50g fresh broccoli
  • 30ml skimmed milk
  • 2 tablespoons light mayonnaise
  • 1 banana

Preparation of the recipe:

  1. Melt the chocolate in a saucepan with a little water for a smooth and creamy texture.
  2. Beat the egg whites until stiff with a mixer for a solid texture.
  3. Add the pinch of salt to the chocolate.
  4. Gently fold the chocolate into the egg whites little by little (the wooden spoon is ideal to avoid breaking the whites).
  5. Put the preparation in 6 mini-ramekins.
  6. Reserve in the fridge for at least 6 hours.
  7. Enjoy as is or with a little grated coconut on top.

Nutritional values:

Calories 192kcal
Protein 5g
carbohydrates 14g
Lipids 7g
