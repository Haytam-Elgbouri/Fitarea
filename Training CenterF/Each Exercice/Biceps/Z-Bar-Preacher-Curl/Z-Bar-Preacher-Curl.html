Z bar preacher curl

The EZ Bar Preacher Curl is a variation of the regular preacher curl, which is done with a straight barbell. The EZ-bar allows for a more comfortable grip, reducing the strain on the wrists and elbows. The preacher bench helps isolate the biceps by keeping the upper arms stationary and eliminating any swinging or cheating that can occur during regular bicep curls.
