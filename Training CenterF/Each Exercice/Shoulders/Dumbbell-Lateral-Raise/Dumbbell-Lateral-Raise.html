Dumbbell lateral raise

The dumbbell lateral raise is an exercise that primarily targets the lateral deltoids, the muscles on the sides of the shoulders. It is an effective isolation exercise for developing shoulder strength, stability, and aesthetics. Balanced shoulder development can help create a proportionate and visually appealing physique.
