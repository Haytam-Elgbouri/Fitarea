Kneeling cable crunch

The first muscle group that works in this exercise is your rectus abdominis, your six-pack muscles. It also helps to work on other core muscles. In order to get the most out of this exercise, it is important that the movement is done in the right shape. It may not be suitable for beginners and people with back injuries.
